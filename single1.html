Busy Buzzbee’s Big Day

Buzzbee was a tiny bumblebee with a big job—collecting nectar for her hive! “Buzz, buzz, zoom!” She zipped to a flower.

One flower… two flowers… THREE! But then… bonk! She flew into a butterfly.

Oooooooooooooooh

“Slow down, Buzzbee!” giggled the butterfly. “Why are you in such a rush?” “I’m working hard for my hive!” said Buzzbee.
“We need honey!” She buzzed off again—up, down, all around! Other bugs were playing tag and napping in the sun.
“Come play!” they called.



But Buzzbee said, “Not yet! I’ve got a job to do!” She visited ten flowers (phew!), filled her nectar pot, and flew back home.
“Buzzbee, you’re amazing!” said the Queen Bee.
“Thanks to you, we have sweet honey today!” Buzzbee smiled and finally took a little nap—with a full tummy and a happy heart.

The End.
