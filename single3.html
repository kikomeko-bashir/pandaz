Tina the Tiny Turtle

Tina was a tiny turtle with a big dream—she wanted to build the tallest sandcastle on Turtle Beach!

She dug with her little flippers… scoop, scoop, scoop. She patted the sand… tap, tap, tap. But whoosh!—the wind blew it down.

But whoosh!—the wind blew it down. “Oh no!” Tina sighed. Along came speedy Sammy the Squirrel. “Too slow, Tina! Just give up!” But Tina shook her tiny head. “Nope! I’m going to try again.” She scooped, tapped, and shaped… all day long. “Want help?” asked Penny the Penguin. “Yes, please!” said Tina.



Together they scooped and stacked. The castle grew taller and taller. At last, it was done! It had towers, doors, and even a moat! Everyone cheered. Sammy’s eyes went wide. “Whoa! You did it!” Tina smiled, tired but happy. “Hard work and friends make dreams come true!”

The End.
