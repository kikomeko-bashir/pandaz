The Silly Sandwich Parade

One sunny day, Benny the Bunny made a peanut butter sandwich. But—oops!—he dropped it, and it stuck to his head!

"Ta-da! I'm a sandwich hat bunny!" Benny giggled. Along came Daisy the Duck. "What’s on your head, Benny?"

"My lunch!" Benny said proudly. Daisy laughed so hard, she hiccuped and quacked!
Then she plopped a jelly toast on her head. Now there were two silly sandwich heads! Next came Freddy the Fox with a banana on his nose. "Is this the food fashion show?" he asked.



"Yes!" Benny and Daisy shouted. "Join the parade!" So they marched—Benny with his sandwich hat, Daisy with jelly hair, and Freddy with his banana nose—down the forest path. The birds laughed. The trees wiggled. Even the clouds chuckled. It was the silliest, yummiest, giggliest parade the forest had ever seen!

The End.
